Ecology and sustainable development

I have taken several courses with a lot of practice along them in topics such as agroecology, bioconstruction, permaculture, medicinal herbs. All these subject pretend sustainable ways of produce food, build houses and treat certain diseases, mitigating the impact on our planet and on our health. On those occasion I got my hand on duty, sowing and reaping variaty of fruit and vegetables, building houses using natural material, or harvasting herbs for selective teas. Most of these experiencies were as a volunteer, exchanging my time for something to eat and a place to sleep. In return I got invaluable knowlegde from real masters and unforgettable moments sharing time with local people and workers who teach us about their customs and perpectives.
